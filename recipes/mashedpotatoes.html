Mashed Potatoes

A simple yet elegant recipe for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes. Waxy ones work better, but any kind will do.
  • 1 stick grass fed butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Peel and wash the potatoes, before putting them in a pot of boiling water for 7 minutes or fork tender.
  2. Once done, remove the spuds to a strainer, with a bowl underneath to catch the runoff, and place a cloth over the strainer to let the potatoes steam for a few minutes more.
  3. Mince the garlic as finely as possible.
  4. Move the potatoes now to a large bowl for mashing. Using a masher, or fork, crush them gently. You may work it as smooth or rough as you like.
  5. Add half the butter and milk, stirring until incorporated. Add the rest and stir once more.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with chives if you like.
